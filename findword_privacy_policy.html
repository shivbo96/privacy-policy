FindWord Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024
Last Updated: December 2024

Welcome to FindWord! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our word puzzle game application ("FindWord" or "the App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Game Data

  • Game Statistics: Your game scores, win/loss records, and playing patterns
  • Game Progress: Your current game state, attempts, and progress
  • Settings Preferences: Audio settings, notification preferences, and other app configurations

1.2 Device Information

  • Device Identifiers: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • App Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and session duration
  • Performance Data: App crashes, errors, and performance metrics

1.3 Device Permissions

  • Notification Permissions: To send game reminders and cooldown notifications
  • Exact Alarm Permissions: For precise timing of notification delivery
  • Vibration Access: For haptic feedback during gameplay
  • Network Access: For ads, analytics, and word validation
  • Wake Lock: To ensure notifications work when device is sleeping

1.4 Analytics Data

  • Usage Analytics: How often you play, which features you use, and gameplay patterns
  • Technical Analytics: App performance, loading times, and technical issues
  • User Behavior: Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app experience

1.5 Advertising Data

  • Ad Interactions: When you interact with rewarded video ads
  • Ad Performance: Ad loading times and completion rates
  • Advertising ID: Device advertising identifier for personalized ads (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Game Functionality

  • Provide and maintain the word puzzle game experience
  • Track your game progress and statistics
  • Implement the cooldown system (1-hour intervals between games)
  • Send notifications when cooldown periods end
  • Save your game settings and preferences
  • Enable ad bypass functionality for cooldown system

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve game features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Optimize app performance and user experience

2.3 Analytics and Monitoring

  • Monitor app performance and stability
  • Track feature usage and user engagement
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics
  • Improve app reliability and user satisfaction

2.4 Advertising

  • Display relevant rewarded video ads
  • Track ad performance and effectiveness
  • Provide ad bypass functionality for cooldown system

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): For analytics, crash reporting, and cloud storage
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Analytics Services: For understanding app usage and performance

3.2 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and property
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activities

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Game data is stored locally on your device
  • Settings and preferences are saved locally
  • No personal data is transmitted without your consent

4.2 Cloud Storage

  • Anonymous analytics data may be stored in Firebase
  • Crash reports and error logs are stored securely
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest

4.3 Security Measures

  • Data encryption for all transmissions
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Access

  • You can view your game statistics within the app
  • You can reset your game progress at any time
  • You can modify your app settings and preferences

5.2 Data Control

  • Disable analytics tracking in app settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Delete local app data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us to request data deletion

5.3 Notification Preferences

  • Enable or disable push notifications
  • Customize notification timing and frequency
  • Manage notification permissions through device settings
